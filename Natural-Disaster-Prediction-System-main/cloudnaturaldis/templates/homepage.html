Vision & Mission

Disasters - natural or human-made are common throughout the world. It is believed that in the recent past there is an increase in their magnitude, complexity, frequency and economic impact. India is at the highest risk of exposure to natural hazards in the world. ML and AI, when combined, can help responders: - track and predict disaster trajectory; - identify location-based urgent needs and most impacted regions;

Objective

To find out ways of prevention of disaster
To identify the use of artificial intelligence in disaster
To Prediction of Natural Disasters & send early warning messages.
Life-saving aid to most impacted or at risk regions
